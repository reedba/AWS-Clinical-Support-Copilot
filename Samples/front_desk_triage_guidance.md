Front Desk Triage Guidance
=========================

Purpose:
--------
To provide front desk staff with clear guidelines for triaging patient concerns and directing them to the appropriate resources at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This guidance applies to all front desk and reception staff.

Guidance:
---------
1. Greet each patient courteously and confirm their identity.
2. Ask the patient to briefly describe their reason for visiting or calling.
3. Use the triage checklist to determine the urgency and type of concern:
   - Medical emergency: Call 911 or direct to the nearest emergency department.
   - Urgent symptoms (e.g., chest pain, shortness of breath): Notify the on-call nurse or provider immediately.
   - Routine appointment or follow-up: Schedule as appropriate.
   - Administrative requests (e.g., records, billing): Direct to the appropriate department.
4. Document the interaction and any actions taken in the patient’s record.
5. Maintain patient privacy and confidentiality at all times.

Triage Checklist (Sample):
--------------------------
- Is the patient experiencing severe pain or distress?
- Are there signs of a medical emergency?
- Does the patient need to speak with a provider today?
- Is this an administrative or clinical concern?

Contact:
--------
For triage support, contact the Lead Receptionist at triage@examplehealth.org.
