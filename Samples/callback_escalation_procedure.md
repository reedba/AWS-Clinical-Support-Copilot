Callback Escalation Procedure
============================

Purpose:
--------
To ensure timely and appropriate escalation of patient callback requests that require urgent or specialized attention at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This procedure applies to all staff handling incoming patient callback requests.

Procedure:
----------
1. Receive and log the callback request in the tracking system.
2. Assess the urgency based on the patient’s concern and clinic guidelines.
3. If the request is routine, assign it to the appropriate staff member for follow-up within 24 hours.
4. If the request is urgent (e.g., symptoms worsening, medication issues), immediately notify the on-call nurse or provider.
5. If the request cannot be resolved within standard timeframes, escalate to the Clinic Manager.
6. Document all actions taken and communications in the patient’s record.

Escalation Levels:
------------------
- Level 1: Routine – Standard follow-up
- Level 2: Urgent – Immediate provider/nurse notification
- Level 3: Unresolved/Complex – Escalate to Clinic Manager

Contact:
--------
For escalation support, contact the Clinic Manager at manager@examplehealth.org.
