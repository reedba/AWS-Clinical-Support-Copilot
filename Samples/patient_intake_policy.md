Patient Intake Policy
=====================

Purpose:
--------
To outline the procedures for collecting and verifying patient information during the intake process at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This policy applies to all front desk and administrative staff responsible for new patient registration.

Policy:
-------
1. All new patients must complete the intake form with accurate demographic and insurance information.
2. Staff must verify the provided information with a valid ID and insurance card.
3. All data must be entered into the electronic health record (EHR) system within 24 hours.
4. Privacy and confidentiality must be maintained at all times in accordance with HIPAA guidelines.
5. Any discrepancies or missing information must be flagged and resolved before the patient’s first appointment.

Procedure:
----------
- Greet the patient and provide the intake form.
- Review the completed form for completeness and legibility.
- Request and verify identification and insurance documents.
- Enter data into the EHR system.
- File physical documents in the secure intake folder.

Contact:
--------
For questions regarding this policy, contact the Clinic Administrator at admin@examplehealth.org.
