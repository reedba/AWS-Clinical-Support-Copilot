Appointment Scheduling SOP
========================

Purpose:
--------
To provide a standardized procedure for scheduling patient appointments at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This SOP applies to all staff responsible for managing the clinic’s appointment calendar.

Procedure:
----------
1. Greet the patient and confirm their identity.
2. Determine the type of appointment required (e.g., new patient, follow-up, specialist referral).
3. Check provider availability in the scheduling system.
4. Offer the earliest available appointment that matches the patient’s needs.
5. Confirm the appointment details with the patient (date, time, provider).
6. Enter the appointment into the scheduling system and provide a confirmation to the patient.
7. Remind the patient of any required documentation or preparation.
8. Document any special requests or accommodations.

Policy:
-------
- All appointments must be scheduled in the electronic system.
- Double-booking is not permitted unless authorized by a supervisor.
- Cancellations and rescheduling must be documented.

Contact:
--------
For questions regarding this SOP, contact the Scheduling Coordinator at schedule@examplehealth.org.
