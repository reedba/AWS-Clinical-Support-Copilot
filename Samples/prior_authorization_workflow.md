Prior Authorization Workflow
===========================

Purpose:
--------
To outline the steps for obtaining prior authorization for medical services or medications at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This workflow applies to all clinical and administrative staff involved in submitting and tracking prior authorization requests.

Workflow Steps:
---------------
1. Identify services or medications requiring prior authorization based on payer guidelines.
2. Collect necessary patient and provider information, including clinical documentation.
3. Complete the payer-specific prior authorization form or online submission.
4. Submit the request to the insurance provider and document the submission date.
5. Track the status of the request daily until a determination is received.
6. Communicate approval or denial to the provider and patient.
7. If denied, review the reason and initiate an appeal if appropriate.
8. Document all actions and communications in the patient’s record.

Best Practices:
---------------
- Use checklists to ensure all required information is included.
- Maintain a log of all prior authorization requests and outcomes.
- Follow up with payers proactively to avoid delays.

Contact:
--------
For workflow support, contact the Insurance Coordinator at insurance@examplehealth.org.
