Privacy and Data Handling Policy
===============================

Purpose:
--------
To establish guidelines for protecting patient privacy and ensuring the secure handling of all health information at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This policy applies to all staff, contractors, and volunteers who access, use, or manage patient data.

Policy:
-------
1. All patient information is confidential and must be protected in accordance with HIPAA and applicable laws.
2. Access to patient data is restricted to authorized personnel only and must be used solely for legitimate clinical or administrative purposes.
3. Electronic health records (EHR) must be accessed using secure, unique user credentials.
4. Physical records must be stored in locked, secure areas when not in use.
5. Patient data must not be shared via unsecured channels (e.g., personal email, unencrypted devices).
6. All data breaches or suspected breaches must be reported immediately to the Privacy Officer.
7. Staff must complete annual privacy and security training.

Procedures:
-----------
- Verify identity before disclosing patient information.
- Use secure methods for transmitting and storing data (e.g., encrypted email, secure cloud storage).
- Shred or securely dispose of physical documents containing patient data.

Contact:
--------
For privacy concerns or to report a breach, contact the Privacy Officer at privacy@examplehealth.org.
