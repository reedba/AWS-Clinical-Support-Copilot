Discharge Follow-Up Checklist
============================

Purpose:
--------
To ensure all necessary follow-up actions are completed after a patient is discharged from Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This checklist applies to all clinical and administrative staff responsible for post-discharge care coordination.

Checklist:
----------
- [ ] Confirm discharge summary is completed and sent to the patient’s primary care provider.
- [ ] Schedule follow-up appointments as needed.
- [ ] Review and reconcile medications with the patient.
- [ ] Provide written and verbal instructions for home care.
- [ ] Arrange for any required home health services or equipment.
- [ ] Ensure patient has contact information for questions or concerns.
- [ ] Document all follow-up actions in the patient’s record.

Best Practices:
---------------
- Use this checklist for every discharged patient to ensure continuity of care.
- Encourage patients to call with any questions or if symptoms worsen.

Contact:
--------
For follow-up support, contact the Care Coordinator at care@examplehealth.org.
