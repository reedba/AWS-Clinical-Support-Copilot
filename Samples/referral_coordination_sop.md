Referral Coordination SOP
========================

Purpose:
--------
To provide a standardized procedure for coordinating patient referrals to external specialists or services at Example Health Clinic.

Scope:
------
This SOP applies to all clinical and administrative staff involved in the referral process.

Procedure:
----------
1. Identify the need for a referral based on provider assessment and patient needs.
2. Obtain patient consent for sharing relevant medical information with the referral provider.
3. Complete the referral form with all necessary patient and clinical details.
4. Send the referral form and supporting documentation to the receiving provider via secure fax, email, or electronic portal.
5. Document the referral in the patient’s health record, including date sent and provider details.
6. Follow up with the receiving provider to confirm appointment scheduling and receipt of records.
7. Notify the patient of the referral status and provide instructions for next steps.
8. Track referral outcomes and update the patient’s record accordingly.

Best Practices:
---------------
- Use a referral log to monitor all outgoing and incoming referrals.
- Ensure timely communication between all parties.
- Protect patient privacy and comply with all data sharing regulations.

Contact:
--------
For referral support, contact the Referral Coordinator at referrals@examplehealth.org.
