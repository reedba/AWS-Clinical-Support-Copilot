# Example Prompts for Each Document Type

## Patient Intake Policy
- "Summarize the key steps required for new patient intake at Example Health Clinic."
- "What information must be verified during the patient intake process?"

## Appointment Scheduling SOP
- "Describe the standard procedure for scheduling a patient appointment."
- "What should staff do if a patient requests an urgent appointment?"

## Callback Escalation Procedure
- "List the escalation levels for patient callback requests."
- "What actions should be taken if a callback request is marked as urgent?"

## Prior Authorization Workflow
- "Outline the workflow for obtaining prior authorization for a medication."
- "What steps should be followed if a prior authorization request is denied?"

## Discharge Follow-Up Checklist
- "What are the required follow-up actions after a patient is discharged?"
- "How should staff document post-discharge follow-up?"

## Privacy and Data Handling Policy
- "What are the main privacy requirements for handling patient data?"
- "How should a data breach be reported according to clinic policy?"

## Front Desk Triage Guidance
- "How should front desk staff triage a patient with urgent symptoms?"
- "What questions should be asked to determine the urgency of a patient’s concern?"

## Referral Coordination SOP
- "Describe the process for coordinating a patient referral to a specialist."
- "What information must be included in a referral form?"

## New Patient Intake Form (JSON)
- "Extract the patient's insurance provider from the intake form."
- "List the emergency contact details for the patient."

## Callback Request (JSON)
- "What is the reason for the callback request?"
- "Is the callback request marked as urgent or routine?"

## Follow-Up Request (JSON)
- "When is the follow-up appointment scheduled?"
- "What type of follow-up is required for the patient?"
